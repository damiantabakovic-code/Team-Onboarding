Step 1: Go to claude.ai
Open claude.ai on your phone or computer. You don't need a paid account to start — the free version works fine.
👋
Step 2: Introduce yourself and the business
Copy and paste the message below as your very first message to Claude. This sets the context for everything that follows.
Copy this prompt
Hi Claude! I'm building an Enagic affiliate marketing business and I want your help creating a content system and personal brand strategy tailored specifically to me. I'm going to paste in my Grand Slam Offer summary in a moment. Before I do, I want you to understand the business model: Enagic sells Kangen water machines (medical-grade water ionisers ranging from $4K-$8K). As an affiliate, I earn commissions when I help someone purchase a machine. What makes this different is that I also mentor my customers — helping them build their own business if they choose to, just like my mentor did for me. My goal is to build a personal brand on [YOUR PLATFORM/S] that attracts the right people, tells my story, and leads them to DM me. I want help with: my content pillars, a weekly posting framework, reel scripts written in my voice, caption templates, and a 30 day content plan. Ready for my offer summary?
📋
Step 3: Paste your Grand Slam Offer summary
After Claude replies, go back to the previous screen and copy your full summary. Paste it into Claude. It will use everything to understand your story, your audience, and what makes you unique.
🗺️
Step 4: Tell Claude about your life and setup
Share the details that make your story visual and specific — where you live or travel, your job, your setup, and any results you've had so far. The more specific, the better your content will be.
Copy this prompt
Here's some context about my life and setup that will help make my content specific and visual: - My background: [YOUR JOB / CAREER] - My story: [1-2 sentences on why you started this] - Where I'm based / how I live: [home, travelling, city, regional, etc.] - My best result so far: [first commission, income milestone, lifestyle change] - The doubts I faced: [what people said or thought when you started] - My partner / family situation: [optional but powerful if relevant] Please use all of this to build me a content system that feels completely specific to my life — not generic online business content.
🚀
Step 5: Ask for your full content system
Once Claude has your offer and your context, use the prompt below to request your complete content system.

Based on everything I've shared, please build me: 1. My 5 core content pillars with a description and content ideas for each 2. A 30 day content plan with specific reel and story ideas for each day Please make everything specific to MY story, MY audience, and MY background — not generic. Write it in a tone that feels natural for someone like me to actually say out loud.
